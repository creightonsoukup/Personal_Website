Interests Unplugged

Currently. I am in training to become a certified Yoga teacher. I have been practicing yoga for about 4 months, and wanted to take my practice to the next level, while also learning how to teach others. When I am not coding or practicing yoga, I am probably outside somewhere. In the summers, I enjoy hiking, backpacking, fly fishing, road and mountain biking, paddle boarding, and climbing. I try to spend one day a week doing something active outdoors. During the winter months, I usually am skiing on the mountain, touring the backcountry, or skinning up to a 10th Mountain Hut.

I am fairly extroverted and am usually with family or friends, either dragging them into the wild or getting a meal in town. I am equally happy to spend the day exploring the city. I love going to art galleries, museums, new restaurants, and shows.
